About Us

Our Automated Waste Classification System is designed to make waste management smarter and more efficient. By using advanced machine learning and image recognition, the system can automatically identify different types of waste such as plastic, paper, metal, glass, e-waste, and compost. This helps users easily segregate waste, encourages recycling, and reduces environmental impact. With a simple image upload, the system predicts the waste type and even suggests recycling possibilities, making it a practical solution for promoting sustainability and eco-friendly living.
